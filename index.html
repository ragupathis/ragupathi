Ragupathi S - Product Guy, Developer, User, Reader, Author

Ragupathi S

    I am a product lead and a product developer with over 4 years of experience working for product based companies.

    Now, I'm leading a team of SDE at DesignQube (IT Sector). Being an early employee in yet another time in my career, DesignQube gave me an opportunity to be a part of the diverse roles. It includes Team Building, Product Ownership, Product Roadmap Planning, Creating Usecases and Personas, Development Planning and Product Developer.

    Previously, I was part of the core team at GUVI Geek Pvt Ltd (www.guvi.in) and was a part of the team that scaled from 1 to 50,000 users over a span of two years. During my time there, I played different roles in the areas of Development, Mentoring, Hiring and Digital Marketing and contributed to the several coding events. Most notably, I had the opportunity to build a recruitment and learning platform for GUVI as a developer.

    Out of curiosity, I built a few experimental products earlier in my career which didn't take up greatly, 1. www.vBookmarks.com- A Crowd-Sourced Social Bookmarking Platform , 2. www.GoodWhale.Club - Challenge for good and 3. https://chennai-startups.github.io - Chennai Startups . I had a great passion towards Infographic (Infographic Blog - www.timepausecreations.wordpress.com) and Online Mentoring (Tech Sharing YouTube Channel - www.youtube.com/timewithragu).

    I have a passion for literature and I indulge myself as a freelance writer and worked as a contributor/editor for several independent blogs and media outlets including YourStory(YS-Tamil). Published a kindle version of Haiku book (அடுத்த மழை எப்போ வரும்?: கவிதை தொகுப்பு) on Amazon. Please feel free to get in touch with me if you have any questions or an interesting story to share.
